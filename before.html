Before the Interview

Preparing for an interview is an extremely important part of the preparation process. From technical training to physiological preparation, there are a lot of small things you can do to be at your best during the interview process, which may include multiple interviews throughout a few days. It requires training from as early as a year before your intended starting day at your desired job. It is very similar to preparing for a sports contest. Winning a marathon, for instance, can require a year of training. But of course, if the contestant is out shape, more years of exercise are needed. If you are serious about getting a job in the software industry, I highly suggest referring to the book “Cracking the Coding Interview” as early as possible. And if you can’t afford it, contact me for pro bono personal training.
