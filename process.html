The Interview Process

It is important to understand that experienced interviewers ask very similar questions over and over again to hundreds of candidates over their careers as a recruiter. When they throw a curve ball at you, a question that you are not so sure about, even if you get just half of it right, you may be doing better than hundreds of other candidates who came before you. It is very similar to a curved grade; getting an A in that test may require you to get just 60% of the test right. So don’t be disappointed if you can’t answer all the hard questions correctly.
