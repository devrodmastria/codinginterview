The Offer and Beyond

When negotiating your salary, it means the company wants to hire you, which also means that there is always more money on the table. Follow the top-down selling approach when they ask how much do you want to get paid. Start with a large figure, 50% more than the average paid for the position you are applying for (you can find average salaries on www.glassdoor.com), and negotiate your way down to a number that both you and the employer agree. Be confident on how great you are and how much the company needs you! Remember, there is always more money on the table.


A brilliant example of that can be found in this article: Silicon Valley engineer negotiated a starting salary from 120k to 250k in just a few weeks.
